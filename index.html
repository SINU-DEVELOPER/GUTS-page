    Zero Fees for those who have GUTS

    Geeta University holds a scholarship test called GUTS (Geeta University Test of Scholarship) to give curious young minds from all around the country an opportunity to study in the most vibrant and diversified academic environment possible. GUTS will be conducted to recognize student’s accomplishments, potential, and talent by allowing them to win scholarships and study in futuristic and cutting-edge programs of their choice at Geeta University. The exam will be based on the syllabus of the relevant qualification examination as well as an aptitude for that topic.
